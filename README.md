## What Even Is EVEDEX?

EVEDEX calls itself a "hybrid exchange," which sounds like marketing fluff until you actually dig into what it means. The short version: orders are matched off-chain (fast, like a CEX) but settled on-chain (transparent and self-custodial, like a DEX). Your funds sit in your smart account wallet, not in EVEDEX's hot wallets. They literally cannot block your withdrawals — which, if you've ever had a CEX freeze your account, sounds pretty good right now.

The whole thing runs on EVENTUM, a custom Layer 3 rollup built on Arbitrum Orbit and secured by Ethereum. That's a lot of tech words, but the practical upshot is: fast execution, near-zero fees, and the security guarantees of Ethereum underneath.
